NEET PG 2024: NBEMS Collects Rs 75 Crore from Exam Fees

The National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) has collected a substantial amount of Rs 75 crore from the examination fees for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test Postgraduate (NEET PG) 2024. This figure was revealed through a Right to Information (RTI) reply, highlighting the financial scale of the exam, which is a crucial gateway for medical graduates seeking postgraduate education in India.

Background on NEET PG 2024 Fees

In a move to provide relief to candidates, NBEMS reduced the examination fee for NEET PG 2024 by Rs 750 across all categories. As of January 1, 2024, candidates from the General and OBC categories are required to pay Rs 3,500, while those from SC, ST, and PWD categories pay Rs 2,500[1][3]. This reduction aims to benefit thousands of candidates appearing for the exam.

Financial Impact

The collection of Rs 75 crore from exam fees underscores the significant financial resources generated by NEET PG. Despite the fee reduction, NBEMS managed to collect a substantial amount, indicating a large number of candidates participating in the exam. This revenue is crucial for NBEMS to conduct quality examinations and support medical education initiatives.

Context and Implications
