MBBS Fees in Georgia for Indian Students

Studying MBBS in Georgia has become an increasingly popular choice among Indian students due to its affordability, quality education, and recognition by prestigious medical councils like the National Medical Commission (NMC) and World Health Organization (WHO). Here's a detailed overview of the MBBS fees in Georgia, along with insights into living costs and other essential considerations.

Overview of MBBS in Georgia

Georgia offers a 5-year MBBS program followed by a 1-year internship, making it a total of 6 years. The course is taught entirely in English, which is beneficial for international students. The eligibility criteria require students to have at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) for general category students and 40% for reserved categories, along with a mandatory NEET score.

MBBS Fees in Georgia

The MBBS fees in Georgia are highly competitive compared to other countries. On average, the annual tuition fees range from INR 2.5 lakh to INR 6.7 lakh. This affordability, combined with the quality of education, makes Georgia an attractive destination for Indian students.

  • The minimum tuition fee is around INR 2.5 lakh per year.
  • The maximum tuition fee can go up to INR 6.7 lakh per year.
  • Some universities may offer scholarships, making it even more affordable for deserving students.

Cost of Living in Georgia

Besides tuition fees, the cost of living in Georgia is also relatively low. Students typically spend around INR 11,000 to INR 18,000 per month, depending on their lifestyle and accommodation choices. Hostel fees range from INR 8,000 to INR 15,000 per month, while food expenses are approximately INR 3,000 to INR 5,000 per month.

Travel and Visa Requirements

Indian students need a student visa to study in Georgia. The application process involves submitting necessary documents like a valid passport, admission letter from the university, and proof of financial support. The visa process usually takes about 4 weeks to complete. Traveling to Georgia is straightforward, with flights available from major Indian cities.

Step-by-Step Admission Process for MBBS in Georgia

The admission process for MBBS in Georgia is streamlined and straightforward. Here’s a breakdown of the steps involved:

  • **Research and Select Universities**: Begin by researching top medical universities in Georgia, such as Tbilisi State Medical University and David Tvildiani Medical University.
  • **Check Eligibility Criteria**: Ensure you meet the eligibility requirements, which typically include a minimum of 50% marks in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) in your 12th standard and a valid NEET score.
  • **Prepare Required Documents**: Gather necessary documents like your 10th and 12th marksheets, NEET scorecard, passport, birth certificate, and medical fitness certificate.
  • **Submit Application**: Fill out the application form available on the university's website and upload the required documents.
  • **Receive Invitation Letter**: Once your application is accepted, you will receive an invitation letter from the university.
  • **Tuition Fee Payment**: Pay the necessary tuition fees to secure your admission.
  • **Apply for Student Visa**: Submit your visa application with documents like your admission letter, passport, and proof of financial support.
  • **Travel to Georgia**: Plan your trip and complete any remaining enrollment formalities upon arrival.

Eligibility Criteria for MBBS in Georgia

To be eligible for MBBS in Georgia, Indian students must meet certain criteria:

  • **Age**: You must be at least 17 years old and not more than 25 years old.
  • **Academic Qualifications**: A minimum of 50% marks in PCB in your 12th standard.
  • **NEET Qualification**: A valid NEET score is mandatory for admission.

These criteria may vary slightly among different universities, so it's important to check the specific requirements for your chosen institution.

MBBS Fees in Georgia and Cost of Living

The cost of studying MBBS in Georgia is relatively affordable compared to other European countries. Here’s an overview of the tuition fees and living expenses:

Tuition fees range from approximately €3,000 to €7,500 per year, depending on the university. For example, Tbilisi State Medical University charges around €7,500 annually, while Akaki Tsereteli University charges €3,000.

The cost of living in Georgia is also relatively low, with monthly hostel fees ranging from €200 to €250. Additionally, students can find affordable accommodation options outside the university, and the overall living expenses are manageable.

Given its affordability and quality education, Georgia is an attractive option for Indian students looking to study MBBS abroad.

Travel and Visa Requirements

After securing admission, the next step is to apply for a student visa:

  • **Visa Application**: Submit your application with required documents like your admission letter, passport, and bank statement proving financial capability.
  • **Travel Arrangements**: Once your visa is approved, book your flight tickets to Georgia. The travel time from India to Georgia is typically around 5-6 hours.

It’s recommended to apply for a visa well in advance to ensure timely processing.

Why Choose Georgia for MBBS?

Georgia offers a unique blend of quality education, cultural exposure, and affordability for Indian students:

  • **Global Recognition**: Georgian medical universities are recognized by WHO and NMC (National Medical Commission), ensuring that your degree is globally accepted.
  • **Practical Training**: Universities in Georgia emphasize practical training, providing students with hands-on experience in medical procedures.
  • **English as Medium of Instruction**: English is the primary language of instruction, making it easier for international students to adapt.

Studying MBBS in Georgia can be a rewarding experience, offering students a strong foundation in medical science and opportunities for further studies or internships.
