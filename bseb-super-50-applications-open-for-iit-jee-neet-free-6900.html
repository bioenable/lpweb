BSEB ‘Super 50’ Applications Open for IIT JEE, NEET Free Coaching

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has announced the opening of applications for its prestigious "BSEB Super 50" program, offering free coaching for students aspiring to appear in the Indian Institute of Technology - Joint Entrance Examination (IIT-JEE) and the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). This initiative, launched in 2023 by Bihar's Chief Minister Nitish Kumar, aims to provide meritorious students with high-quality educational resources to excel in these competitive exams.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the BSEB Super 50 program, students must either have passed the Class X board exam or be appearing candidates. Additionally, students currently in Class 10 who wish to study in Bihar Board-affiliated schools for Class 11 can apply. Those already in Class 11 can also apply for the coaching program, which will prepare them for the exams in the subsequent years[1][3].

Features of the Program

  • Free Residential and Non-Residential Coaching: The program offers both residential and non-residential coaching options, ensuring that students from diverse backgrounds can benefit.
  • Expert Faculty: The coaching program features teachers from top coaching centers across the country, providing students with expert guidance and specialized classes[1][3].
  • Digital Classrooms: All classrooms are equipped with air-conditioned facilities and digital boards to enhance the learning experience[3].
  • Regular Tests and Health Check-ups: Regular tests are conducted twice a month, and separate doubt-clearing sessions are held to improve understanding. Additionally, free health check-ups are available for students in the hostels[3].

Application Process

Applications for the 2025-27 batch are open from February 22 to March 12, 2025. Interested students can apply through the official website at coaching.biharboardonline.com[1][4]. The program is available in nine divisional districts of Bihar: Muzaffarpur, Patna, Chhapra, Darbhanga, Saharsa, Purnia, Bhagalpur, Gaya, and Munger[1][4].

Performance Highlights

The first batch of the BSEB Super 50 program has shown impressive results, with all students qualifying for the JEE Main 2025. Four students achieved a score of 99 percentile or higher: Yash Raj (99.20), Sunny Kumar (99.18), Ashish Kumar (99.10), and Sachin Kumar (99)[3][5]. This success underscores the effectiveness of the program in preparing students for competitive exams.

Conclusion

The BSEB Super 50 initiative represents a significant step forward in providing equal educational opportunities to talented students from Bihar. By offering free coaching and leveraging top-notch educational resources, the program aims to empower students to achieve their full potential in IIT-JEE and NEET exams. As applications for the new batch are now open, aspiring students have a unique opportunity to benefit from this esteemed program.
