NEET Biology Quiz

Prepare for your medical entrance exams with this comprehensive Biology quiz designed to test your knowledge of key concepts covered in NEET examinations.

1. Which of the following organelles is known as the "power house" of the cell?
Golgi apparatus
Endoplasmic reticulum
Mitochondria
Lysosomes
✓ Correct! Mitochondria are known as the powerhouse of the cell.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Mitochondria.
2. Which of the following is NOT a part of the human digestive system?
Liver
Thymus
Pancreas
Gall bladder
✓ Correct! Thymus is part of the lymphatic system, not the digestive system.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Thymus.
3. Which of the following hormones is produced by the thyroid gland?
Insulin
Testosterone
Thyroxine
Adrenaline
✓ Correct! Thyroxine is produced by the thyroid gland.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Thyroxine.
4. Which of the following diseases is caused by a virus?
Tuberculosis
Malaria
Dengue fever
Cholera
✓ Correct! Dengue fever is caused by a virus transmitted by mosquitoes.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Dengue fever.
5. Which of the following is the correct sequence of the phases of mitosis?
Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase
Prophase, Anaphase, Metaphase, Telophase
Metaphase, Prophase, Anaphase, Telophase
Metaphase, Anaphase, Prophase, Telophase
✓ Correct! The correct sequence is Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase.
6. Which of the following blood vessels carries oxygenated blood?
Pulmonary artery
Pulmonary vein
Superior vena cava
Inferior vena cava
✓ Correct! Pulmonary vein carries oxygenated blood from lungs to the heart.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Pulmonary vein.
7. Which of the following best describes the function of nephrons in the human body?
They are responsible for blood cell production
They filter blood and form urine
They help in digestion of proteins
They transmit nerve impulses
✓ Correct! Nephrons filter blood and form urine in the kidneys.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is They filter blood and form urine.
8. Which of the following is the site of protein synthesis in a cell?
Ribosomes
Mitochondria
Chloroplast
Golgi apparatus
✓ Correct! Ribosomes are the sites of protein synthesis in a cell.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Ribosomes.
9. Which of the following is the primary causative agent of malaria?
Plasmodium
Trypanosoma
Leishmania
Entamoeba
✓ Correct! Plasmodium is the parasite that causes malaria.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Plasmodium.
10. Which of the following is NOT a function of the liver?
Production of bile
Storage of glycogen
Detoxification of harmful substances
Production of insulin
✓ Correct! Insulin is produced by the pancreas, not the liver.
✗ Incorrect! The correct answer is Production of insulin.
