Test your knowledge in Biology with this comprehensive quiz based on NEET exam patterns. Perfect for students aged 15-25 preparing for medical entrance exams.

1. Which of the following is NOT a function of the kidney?

✗ Incorrect! Bile is produced by the liver, not the kidney.

✗ Incorrect! Herbert Spencer coined the phrase "survival of the fittest" after reading Darwin's work.

✗ Incorrect! Mitochondria generate ATP through cellular respiration.
