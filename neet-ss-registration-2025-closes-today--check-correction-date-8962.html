NEET SS 2025 Registration Closes Today: Key Details on Correction Date, Exam Schedule, and Editable Fields

The registration window for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test-Super Specialty (NEET SS) 2025 closes today, February 24, 2025. Eligible candidates must complete their applications by 11:55 PM on the official website, natboard.edu.in[1][2][3]. Here are the essential details regarding the correction window, exam schedule, and what can be edited during the correction period:

Correction Window

- Dates: The correction window will be open from February 27 to March 3, 2025[1][2][4]. - Editable Fields: Candidates can edit most fields in their application form except for their name, test city, nationality, mobile number, and email ID[3][4].

Exam Schedule

- Exam Dates: The NEET SS 2025 exam will be conducted on March 29 and 30, 2025[1][2]. - Exam Format: The exam will be in a group-based format, consisting of 150 questions, with a total duration of 2 hours and 30 minutes (150 minutes)[1]. - Shifts: The exam will be held in two shifts: the first shift from 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM and the second shift from 2:00 PM to 4:30 PM[1][2].

Admit Cards

- Release Date: Admit cards for the exam are scheduled to be released on March 25, 2025[1][2].

Additional Correction Window

- Dates: A final edit window for correcting deficient or incorrect images (photograph, signature, thumb impression) will be available from March 11 to March 13, 2025[3][4].

Registration Process

To register, candidates must visit the official NBEMS website, create an account, fill out the application form, upload necessary documents, and pay the examination fee of Rs 3500[2][5]. After completing the registration, candidates should download and print the confirmation page for future reference.

This information is crucial for candidates preparing for the NEET SS 2025 exam, as it is the sole entrance test for admissions into DM, MCh, and DrNB Super Specialty courses[5].
